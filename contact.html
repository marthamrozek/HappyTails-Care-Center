Contact Us


Adopt don't shop!

Thank you for considering HappyTails Adoption Haven for your furry companionship needs. We're here to assist you in providing loving homes for cats and dogs. If you have any questions about our adoption process, want to inquire about a specific pet, or if you're interested in volunteering, please don't hesitate to reach out.

Our team is dedicated to ensuring a seamless and joyful adoption experience. Feel free to use the contact form on the "Contact Us" webpage, drop us an email or give us using the Contact Information at the bottom of this page. We look forward to hearing from you and helping you find your pawwwfect new furry friend!


Do you have a pet to give away? We can help.

Making the decision to rehome a beloved pet can be a challenging one, and at HappyTails Adoption Haven, we understand and empathize. If you find yourself in a situation where you need to find a new loving home for your furry friend, we are here to help. Our compassionate team is dedicated to facilitating a seamless and caring transition for pets in need. By partnering with us, you can ensure that your pet will be placed in a responsible and loving environment. We work tirelessly to match pets with families who can provide the care and attention they deserve.

To discuss rehoming options, please reach out to us through the contact form on our "Have a pet to give away" webpage, send us an email, or give us a call using the Contact Information at the bottom of this page. Your pet's well-being is our priority, and we are committed to assisting you during this challenging time.
